💡 My Journey into Tech

My path to software engineering has been unconventional but rewarding. With a background in Portuguese Language and International Public Administration, I discovered my passion for technology during internships at major tech companies in China.

Working as an operations and product intern, I became fascinated by algorithms, data structures, and the logic behind software features. This curiosity led me to pursue a Master's in Computer Science at Northeastern University, where I've been maintaining a 3.9 GPA while working on cutting-edge projects in AI and cloud computing.

My diverse background—combining language skills, public policy thinking, and technical expertise—gives me a unique perspective on building user-centered, scalable systems that solve real-world problems.
