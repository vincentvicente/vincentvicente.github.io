Journey

My Transition into Software Engineering

My journey into software engineering was largely influenced by my previous internship experiences. During my time working in major tech companies in China as an operations and product intern, I developed a deep interest in understanding algorithms, data structures, and the underlying logic of various features. This curiosity led me to explore programming further, ultimately inspiring my transition into software engineering. The combination of my hands-on experience in product management and my growing knowledge in software development has provided me with a unique perspective that fuels my passion for building efficient, user-centered systems.
